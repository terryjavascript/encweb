ABOUT US

CEO GREETING

EFFICIENCY, INNOVATION, & HUMAN RELATIONSHIPS

I would like to present our profile to you,
which will give you a better understanding
of our company, our people, and our services.


ENC,Inc.is an aggressive and competitive ocean and airfreight forwarder that was founded in 1984 with experienced management at every level. Throughout its history, ENC, Inc. has maintained its excellence in the forwarding industry by adhering to the company’s core values of dedication, confidence, and perseverance. We have also combined modern technology with a fundamental, back-to-basics approach to our business, resulting in a highly-efficient transportation network system. We believe that these principles, when combined with imagination and a keen sense of understanding, will allow for continued growth and progress.

Freight forwarding is a people-oriented business and the ability to respond quickly depends largely on the cooperation of individuals. It is ENC, Inc.’s objective to constantly search for better and more cost-effective measures to deliver results to our customers. We promise to provide quality service, from day-to-day operations to helping our customers with their specific needs.

ENC,Inc.has dealt with adversity in the past, and we are sure to face more challenges in the future. However, our employees will continue to be aggressive and innovative in adapting to the dynamic business world around us.We will add offices where the demand exists for a quality international transportation service. We will strive to attract the best overseas agents and work towards establishing long-term relationships that are key essentials to success for us and our clients. We will continue to evaluate acquisition opportunities of well-established companies in order to complement our strategic goals. Finally, we will continue with our mission of expanding and continually improving our services to meet the needs of our customers throughout the world.


- Kwang Yul Choi / CEO & President
