COMPANY INTRODUCTION

Who are we?


Since our inception in 1984, ENC, Inc.’s management has continually refined its mission statement to be proactive in responding to internal challenges as well as to changes within the industry. With 18 branch offices worldwide, ENC, Inc. has built a reputation of excellence. Our experienced management understands the value of our customers and the commitment of our employees in contributing to the company’s unprecedented success. ENC, Inc.’s foundation of powerful leadership, knowledge, and hard work allows us to provide top quality service at reasonable costs to all of our clients.

ENC, Inc. values our customers and employees because these two cornerstones provide the infrastructure needed to build and expand our company. As we grow internationally, we also understand the importance of innovation in helping us adapt to the ever-changing business environment. We firmly believe that evaluation and adaptability are critical to our success. As we have done in the past, we will continue to challenge our leadership and test our knowledge in order to maintain our competitive edge. Above all, our mission is to continually improve and strengthen our capabilities to provide the highest performance at affordable costs while meeting the needs and standards of our customers throughout the world. We will benchmark our performance, standards, and solutions to lead this dynamic and evolutionary industry.
