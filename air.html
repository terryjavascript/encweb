SERVICES

AIR FREIGHT

A VARIETY OF AIR FREIGHT TRANSPORTATION SERVICES

Rapid and secure air transportation services with worldwide scheduled consolidations and door-to-door delivery.

Our knowledge and experience allows us to provide our clients with a variety of optimum air freight transportation services– including consolidation, bonded transportation, customs clearance and air carriage for our customers’ import and export cargo.


PROJECT CARGO

Our experience in more than 50 air freight projects allows us to quickly identify the most efficient routes and service options. We will utilize our network to ensure timely delivery of our customers’ project cargo to its destination at the most competitive rates on the market.


CONSOLIDATION/DECONSOLIDATION

Our network branch offices and agent companies allow us to offer dependable worldwide scheduled consolidation/deconsolidation services to a variety of customers, including small to medium importers and exporters. We guarantee shortened lead times, and we also provide the security and reliability needed in today’s business world.
